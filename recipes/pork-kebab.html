Pork Kebab

Description

Easy to make, perfect for outside grilling and drinking some beers with friends

Goes well with pretty much anything, salads and homemade potato chips are best

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds of pork neck
  • salt to taste
  • freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • olive oil
  • 2 medium onions

Steps

  1. 24 hours before cooking, marinate the meat
  2. Add salt, black pepper, olive oil and sliced onion to chopped pork
  3. Place in plastic bag in fridge for 24 hours
  4. Start up the charcoal bbq, until coals are all grey/red
  5. Put the chunks of pork on long scewers and place on bbq
  6. turn every 2 minutes until internal temperature 160 and done
