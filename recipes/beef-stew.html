Beef Stew

Description

Ultimate cold weather food! Chunks of well-marbled beef are seared in a hot pan, then gently braised with garlic and onions in a rich wine-based broth

After a few hours in the oven, the meat becomes meltingly tender and enveloped in a deeply flavourful sauce

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds of boneless beef chuck
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 medium yellow onions
  • 7 cloves garllic
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1.5 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cups dry red wine
  • 4 large carrots
  • 1 pound of boiled white potatoes

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325F
  2. Pat beef dry and season with salt and pepper. Brown the meat and transfer to large plate
  3. Cook onion and garlic for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally
  4. Add tomato paste and cook one more minute
  5. Add the beef,scook for 5 minutes
  6. Add beef broth and wine, cover pot with lid and braise for 2 hours
  7. Add carrots and potatoes, put back in oven for 1 hour and done
