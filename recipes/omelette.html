Omelette

Description

Super easy quick omelette, tasty and nutritious!

All the good ingredients, fits the macros well

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 0.5 cup onion
  • 0.5 cup green pepper
  • 1 cup mushrooms, chopped
  • 4 slices of ham
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat scillet to medium high
  2. Chop onions, green pepper and mushrooms
  3. Cook veggies for 5 minutes
  4. Chop ham, add to veggies and cook for 2 minutes
  5. Take out the mix, set on plate
  6. Add eggs in and cook until scrambled
  7. Take eggs out and put on plate with the mix, done
